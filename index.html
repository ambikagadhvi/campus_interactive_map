Welcome to the College Coordination App

This app is designed to streamline communication and coordination among students, staff, and event organizers at our college. Whether you need to find your way around campus, sign up for events, or get in touch with the right department, this app has you covered.

Explore the features to enhance your campus experience!

Key Features:

  • Interactive campus map to navigate with ease.
  • Sign up for events and volunteer opportunities.
  • Stay updated with event schedules and announcements.
